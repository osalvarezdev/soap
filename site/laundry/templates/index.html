Laundry Tips and Tricks

1. Always sort your clothes before washing

Sorting your clothes by fabric type, color, and level of stain can help preserve your clothes and avoid mishaps during laundry (i.e white shirts getting stained)

2. How to remove different types of stains

Blood = For fresh blood, soak it in cold water before laundry whereas for dried blood soak it in warm water before laundry.

Red Wine = Soak it some white vinegar with some light laundry detergent then after 20 minutes wash.

Coffee = Soak it in hot water before laundry.

3. Not every fabric is machine or hand-washable

Know when to dry clean for fabrics like leather and suede or even oil grease stains to avoid damaging it.

4. Prep your clothes before washing

Zip zippers, close whatever is necessary except when dealing with buttons as closing buttons can damage the buttons themselves.

5. Be very gentle when dealing with baby clothes

Normal detergents can irritate babies' skins so use mild soaps and avoid softeners.

6. Don't forget to read the clothes' tags to check what fabric it is.

Some fabrics like wool can tolerate dry cleaning and hand-washing like wool and others but can't tolerate machine washing because of its delicate nature.
